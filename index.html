About

I study snakes to uncover how chemical cues shape predator–prey interactions and communication. My work integrates behavioral experiments, chemical analyses, and field ecology to reveal novel defense strategies in reptiles.

Currently, I am a PhD candidate at Waseda University (Graduate School of Advanced Science and Engineering) and a JSPS DC1 Research Fellow.

Research Interests

  • Chemical communication & predator avoidance
  • Behavioral ecology of reptiles
  • Indirect defense mechanisms
  • Biodiversity surveys & conservation
